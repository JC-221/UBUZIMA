UBUZIMA HUB Platform

INFORMATION

This platform was established by a youth Rwandan, when he was in university of Rwanda, after realising how some of rwandan youth have false information about their lives especially about HIV/AIDS and STDs

He came up with a solution of how they can access true information, then he found this platform UBUZIMA Hub

young Rwandans spend more time on the internet wasting their time, but now they will use this platform to get the information to save their life.

This not for youth only, also evey person can access this platform to get the information he/she want about our life.

As young rwandans have many places where they meet to discuss or study about certain thing like:

  • schools
  • youth centers
  • etc

We work hand in hand with these places to which their activities have included time to help youth accessing these knowledge

Learn skills

  • HIV Prevention
  • Sexual health education
  • Mental health awareness
  • Menstrual health education

Missions

  • To educate young people about HIV/AIDS prevention and awareness
  • To create a stigma-free platform where youth can freely learn and ask questions
  • To promote sexual health education in a safe and engaging environment
  • To provide accessible health education for all young people, including those with disabilities
  • To raise awareness about mental health and emotional well-being among youth
  • To encourage learning through interactive quezzes and fun activities
  • To empower youth to take control of their health, knowledge, and future
  • To improve menstrual health education and awareness among young people
  • To to support inclusive learning using accessibility features such as voice assistance, subtitles, and text resizing
